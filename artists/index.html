Anna Comella

Anna is a Boston-based artist inspired by urban, gritty environments and the artist communities around her. She has a studio in Boston's oldest artist community, Fort Point, as well as in Boston's South End, another mecca for artists. Belonging to both of these communities provides a rich environment of art and connection with fellow artists.

Both areas have undergone enormous changes in the past 5-10 years and she uses these changes to inspire her work. She sees this reinvention as a way to challenge myself as an artist and to experiment with new ways of creating art.

Anna was originally trained in printmaking but has experimented with metals, sculpture, jewelry making, collage, acrylic painting, encaustic, and recently with mosaic fabrication. No matter what the medium or size, her work is always comprised of layers of abstract images and colors. Her intent is for the viewer to find his or her own connection and meaning in the work.
