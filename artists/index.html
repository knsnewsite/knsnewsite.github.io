Artists on Display

Kent Newton Salon showcases artwork from various artists in and around the Boston area that are available for purchase. Don't hesitate to request more info about them during your next salon visit.

Jenn Sherr

With no range to her work, Jenn’s art has been featured many times in the Boston Globe and in the “What's Hot” pages of Stuff Magazine. Boston Magazine has included Jenn Sherr Designs in the “8-Key Items for Fall” and she appears regularly in South End News.

October 2007 Jenn was a finalist in the nationwide Red Bull Art of the Can competition for the unique boots that she enhanced with the Red Bull Logo. Not a fan of the Red Bull? Jen will customize a collage just for you on any theme; bracelets, handbags, shoes, furniture, or an item of your choice.

Along with being a Boston Certified Artist she is also an active member of the Boston Arts.

Interested in learning her trade? Her classes are held in her studio in the South End and at various schools in Boston.
