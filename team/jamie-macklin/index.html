Jamie Macklin

Junior Stylist

James J. Macklin IV “Jamie” has been in the design and cosmetology world for the past 15 years.

Beginning his career as and interior designer, Jamie attended the prestegious Parsons School of Design in New York City and upon returning to Boston, worked for some of the most noted Interoir Design firms on the east coast. Realizing his passon for design wasn’t limited to designing fantastic spaces, but also a desire to provide immediate results and have continued interaction with clients, Jamie attended cosmetology school.

Having his education of color, function, balance, and structure mixed with his continued education in color, cutting, and contemporary and classic styling, his clients appreciate the attention to detail they recieve. Jamie believes that hair is an expression of one’s self and being able to listen to the desires of a client, consulting clearly to find the best style, and then executing flawless, managable hair is paramount to his clients’ happiness.

Jamie has received continuing education in Wella International, Coppola Keratin Treatment, Kerastase, and various training with some of Boston’s top stylists.
