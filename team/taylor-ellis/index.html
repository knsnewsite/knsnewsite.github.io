Taylor Ellis has been an accomplished hairstylist for over 13 years.

Before joining the team at Kent Newton Salon she was not only a stylist, but also the salon manager and director of education at the distinguished James Joseph Salon on Newbury Street for over ten years.

Taylor has trained with Vidal Sassoon, Bumble and Bumble, Goldwell, Wella and Cosmoprof. She is currently working towards a certification from the American Board of Certified Hairstylists.

Taylor also serves as the creative director for the Boston Celtic’s Cheerleaders. She has been the lead stylist for numerous fashion shows, including those for Oscar de la Renta, Carolina Herrera, Giuliana Teso, Akris, Diesel, Neiman Marcus, and Saks Fifth Avenue.

Taylor’s work has also been featured by various media, including the Improper Bostonian, Stuff@Night, Boston Globe and Channel 7/WCVB-Boston.
