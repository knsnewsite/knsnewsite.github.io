Brittany has been in the industry since 2008, when she began her career at top salons Diego Boston on Newbury Street and El Coco Aveda. She was trained in Loreal Color, simply smooth Keritan Treatment, Aveda Color, and Avanti Hair Treatment, among many more cutting and color techniques.

Before starting at Kent Newton Salon, Brittany worked at Dellaria Newbury Street, where she worked with New England's own Patriots Cheerleaders and Dirty Water Media. She also had experience and trained in Wella color and products, Nioxen, Bumble and Bumble, Morrocian Oil, and Phyto.

Brittany has a true passion for all things hair! Making someone feel as beautiful as they look is her main goal with every visit. She finds such healing in her client's happiness!!
