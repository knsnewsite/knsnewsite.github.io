Katie McDaniel Stylist

Katie McDaniel has been in the beauty industry for 9 years. She built her business in Nashville, Tennessee before moving to Boston and joining the Kent Newton Salon team. Having found her calling in the salon environment, she has trained under a renowned Colorist and Educator for Redken. Her compassion and love of people make her a trustworthy stylist that listens and understands her clients every need.

Katie is a Redken Certified Colorist and has extensive experience with numerous haircacare lines including, Kerastase, Shu Uemura, Bumble and Bumble, Davines, Morrocan Oil, and Kevin Murphy

“I love the shine and luxury of rich, gorgeous color. Pair that with the clean lines of a fresh haircut and it is magic. Being able to create dimension and a one of a kind look for someone is a great feeling. Knowing that your hair is fabulous makes anyone walk a little taller!”

Katie’s commitment to staying current with the latest styles and trends helps her clients leave grinning form ear to ear.
