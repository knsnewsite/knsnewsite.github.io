Kimberly Griffin

Stylist

Kimberly, our Blonde Specialist, has been in the style industry for 10+ years. From bridal up dos and color corrections to photo shoots and men's cuts, she enjoys getting her hands into all things hair! After being educated by many of the industry's top stylists and working with organizations like Intercouiffure, her creativity is always growing.

Always on the prowl for the latest and greatest hair and fashion trends, her training has kept her well versed in highlighting and balayage techniques but always enjoys a rich single process. Her focus is on blondes, color corrections and extensions. She is well seasoned in a variety of different hair extention application techniques that give women the hair of their dreams.

Widely known for the creativity and flair she puts into every person that sits in her chair, she's constantly striving to listen and deliver a fun filled experience that leaves you with a beautiful look, a full heart and a big smile.
